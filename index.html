About Me

My name is Jordon Bowen and I was presented to the world of mere mortals on the day of May 29th, 1994. With that being said, the earth will be mine by the time I have reached the very tinder age of 30. Giving Humanity 6 years to vacate the premisis or their demise will be imminent. If not vacated within the specified time frame, you will be forced to join my legion or work in the nearest Build a Bear Workshop with the other softies. Heed my warning for it is my last and final.

Among my many accomplishments in my 24 years of life, my prized and most noteworthy possession would be the red light saber I took from Darth Vader's cold dead hands as I slayed him for the Rebel Alliance. I am your saviour. The Dark Side has taken a tole on me however, and I feel the light fading within me. Only the true nature of the force knows which way my alligiance will sway.
